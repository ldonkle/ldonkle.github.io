Residential Drafting Services

 

Virginia Drafting & Detailing LLC provides architectural drawings of new residential homes and additions. We have been in the residential drafting industry since 2006, and guarantee fast, accurate drawings at a competitive price.

We can provide:

  • Architectural Floor Plans
  • Elevations
  • Roof Plans
  • Details
  • Sections
  • Electrical Plans
  • 3D Perspectives
  • Any scale / paper size
  • Free estimates!

We draft everything in Revit Architecture, which means we will produce a 3D model of your project, and can include 3D images of the exterior/ interior, virtual walkthroughs, the 3D file itself (Viewable in Autodesk's free File Viewer) and also export any 2D AutoCad files you need.
